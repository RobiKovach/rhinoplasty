    Dr. Mesut Pasha

    Dr. Mesut Pasha is the Head of Otorhinolaryngology at Kantonsspital Baselland, specializing in both aesthetic and functional rhinoplasty, including preservation, structural, and revision procedures using Piezo technology. Renowned for his expertise, he utilizes advanced, minimally invasive techniques to improve precision and recovery.
    Dr. Pasha is committed to delivering personalized, high-quality results for
    his patients, ensuring optimal outcomes
    in every case.

    Prof. Dr. Fazıl Apaydın

    Prof. Dr. Fazıl Apaydın, a renowned facial plastic surgeon, is a professor at
    the Department of Otorhinolaryngology
    at Ege University, Turkey. Leading
    the Division of Facial Plastic Surgery since 1999, he is European board certified
    and
    has served as the past president
    of the European Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery. With over 24 years of global experience in teaching, lecturing,
    and performing surgeries, Prof. Apaydin also holds key positions in multiple international organizations and has contributed extensively
    to academic literature in the field.

    Prof. Dr. med. Can Cedidi

    As the Chief of Plastic, Reconstructive,
    and Aesthetic Surgery in one of Germany's largest hospitals, and a Professor at the University of Göttingen and Witten/
    Herdecke, brings unmatched expertise.
    His training spans top institutions like Heidelberg University, Hannover Medical School, and The Mayo Clinic. His expertise in microsurgical reconstruction
    is renowned globally. Prof. Dr. med. Can Cedidi
    is the visionary behind the renowned SOAP Congress, showcasing
    the world’s top plastic surgeons and cutting-edge techniques.

    Dr. med. Timur Taskesen

    As a dermatologist and dermatosurgeon
    in Zurich, Dr Timur Taskesen brings
    a unique perspective to rhinoplasty, enhancing
    outcomes through advanced skin treatments.
    By optimizing skin quality and managing scarring with laser therapy and bioregenerative techniques, he helps ENT and plastic surgeons achieve more refined, natural-looking results. Timur Taskesen will share how dermatology
    can add value to rhinoplasty, from pre-surgery skin prep to post-op recovery.

    Prof. Dr. med. Mohamed Amin

    An Associate Professor at University College Dublin and Consultant Otolaryngologist at
    Mater Misericordiae University Hospital, Dublin, Dr. Amin
    brings a wealth of expertise in sinus disease and septorhinoplasty surgery.
    With fellowship training in London and active roles
    in the European Rhinologic Society and
    European Academy of Facial Plastic Surgery,
    he's a recognized leader
    in his field.

    Dr. Antonio Sousa Vieira

    António Sousa Vieira, an otorhinolaryngologist since 1990, specializes in rhinology, focusing
    on functional and aesthetic nose treatments.
    A frequent international speaker and author
    of several articles,
    he promotes customized rhinoplasty
    to enhance patients' quality of life.
    His expertise combines artistic precision with advanced surgical techniques, refining
    thousands of cases.
